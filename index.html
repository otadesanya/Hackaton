How this industry's customers typically respond iNPS asks one question: "How likely are you to recommend us?" on a 0–10 scale. Customers who answer 9–10 are Promoters — they're loyal and drive growth. 7–8 are Passives — satisfied but not advocates. 0–6 are Detractors — at risk of churning and sharing negative word-of-mouth. The colored bar shows the typical split across companies in this industry benchmark.
Promoter / Passive / Detractor distribution across the benchmark — each color shows what % of customers fall in that group

Text iQ · XM Discover
# Theme Volume iThe percentage of open-text responses in this benchmark that mentioned this theme. Higher volume means more customers are writing about it — but volume alone doesn't indicate whether it's a problem or a positive. Sentiment iText iQ classifies each mention as Positive, Negative, or Mixed based on the language used. Negative + high volume = the highest-priority themes to address first. Trend iWhether mentions of this theme are growing, stable, or declining over time across the benchmark. Rising negative themes are early warning signals — worth building listening programs around before they peak.
